3 Days Masai Mara Safari

📅 3 DAYS / 2 NIGHTS 👥 MIN 2 PEOPLE 🏕️ TENTED CAMP 🎯 GAME DRIVES

Day by Day

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1
Nairobi → Masai Mara

Morning pickup from your Nairobi hotel. Drive through the Great Rift Valley escarpment to Masai Mara. Afternoon game drive with chances to see lion, cheetah, and elephant. Sundowner drinks on the plains. Dinner and overnight at camp.

Day 2
Full Day Game Drives

Early morning game drive before sunrise to catch predators on the hunt. Return to camp for breakfast. Afternoon game drive into the heart of the Mara ecosystem. Optional hot air balloon at extra cost. Bush dinner under the stars.

Day 3
Masai Mara → Nairobi

One last morning game drive. Visit a Maasai village for a cultural experience. Drive back to Nairobi arriving late afternoon. Drop-off at hotel or JKIA airport.

Our Story

Born from a Love of Africa

Adventures Connect Ltd was founded in 2010 by a team of passionate East African naturalists and travel specialists who believed that every traveller deserves to experience Africa the way it was meant to be — raw, real, and deeply personal.

From our humble beginnings with a single safari vehicle in the Masai Mara, we've grown to become one of East Africa's most trusted safari operators, with a fleet of custom 4×4 vehicles, partnerships with over 50 premium lodges and tented camps, and a team of 30+ expert guides across Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, and Uganda.

Our mission is simple: to create unforgettable, responsible safari experiences that connect people with wildlife, culture, and the timeless landscape of East Africa.

FAQs

Common Questions

Do I need a visa to visit Kenya? +
Kenya now operates an Electronic Travel Authorisation (eTA) system. Most nationalities can apply online at etakenya.go.ke before travel. Our team can guide you through the process.
What is the best time for a safari? +
Year-round! But for the Great Migration in Kenya/Tanzania, July–October is peak season. For gorilla trekking in Rwanda, June–September and December–February offer the best conditions.
Are your safaris family-friendly? +
Absolutely. We have dedicated family safari packages with child-appropriate activities, family-sized vehicles, and accommodation that caters to all ages. Minimum age for gorilla trekking is 15.
What's included in the price? +
Our packages include accommodation, meals as stated, park fees, game drives, and an expert guide. International flights, travel insurance, visas, and gratuities are not included.
